All about SM's Nct 127

Read time: 5 minutes

NCT 127 is a sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT, formed by SM Entertainment in 2016. Known for their diverse music styles, including hip-hop, R&B, and EDM, NCT 127 showcases energetic performances and experimental sounds. The group's name refers to the longitude of Seoul, symbolizing their global ambitions.

"I love taeyong!" - shan,2024

With a rotational lineup concept, NCT 127 initially debuted with seven members, later expanding. Members like Taeyong, Mark, and Jaehyun are central figures. Their songs, like "Cherry Bomb" and "Kick It," have cemented them as global icons, blending sharp choreography with genre-bending music.

NCT 127 is a dynamic K-pop group known for its powerful performances and genre-blending music. As a sub-unit of NCT, they aim to represent Seoul's cultural vibrancy, with "127" signifying the city's longitude. Their discography includes global hits like "Limitless" and "Regular," combining unique visuals, choreography, and experimental sounds.
